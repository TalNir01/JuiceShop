# Privacy Policy

**Description**

Read our privacy policy

Types: `Good Practice` ,`Tutorial`, `Good For Demos`

## Background

I have asked claude :what are websites privacy polices and where might I find them?

## Solution

Go to `Account`, create (register) a new user and log in with him.

Then after being logged in please reclick the `Account` and then a new menu bar is displayed.

It had `Privacy & Security` section, which had a link to the `Privacy Policy`

Url is `http://127.0.0.1:3000/#/privacy-security/privacy-policy`
